Reconsider Travel to border regions
Jordan is generally stable relative to its neighbors but faces persistent threats from terrorism and regional spillover from the Syria and Israel-Gaza conflicts. The Jordanian security services maintain high alert nationwide. Amman and Aqaba are lower-risk urban environments. The Syrian border to the north and the Iraqi border to the east are assessed as HIGH risk — avoid entirely.

3 · COUNTERINTELLIGENCE
NCSC · DoDD 5240.06 · OSAC
📡
Foreign Intelligence Service Activity — ELEVATED
Jordan hosts significant intelligence presence from multiple regional and global actors. Hotel rooms, business meeting venues, and communications in Amman are considered potentially monitored. Assume no expectation of privacy in any facility.
🤝
Elicitation Risk
Be alert to unsolicited contact from new acquaintances at hotels, conferences, or social settings who express unusual interest in your work, employer, or business purpose. Do not discuss organizational details in public.
🏨
Hotel & Facility Security
Do not leave laptops, phones, or work documents in hotel rooms unattended. Hotel safes are not secure — assume room access by third parties is possible. Use hotel business center computers only for non-sensitive tasks.

Transportation
